Joe Rogan Grills Elon Musk on His Plans for Twitter

By EMToast Staff
December 15, 2022

Hi, I'm Joe Rogan and today I have the one and only Elon Musk on the podcast. Elon, it's great to have you here.

Elon: Thanks for having me, Joe. It's always a pleasure to be on your show.

Joe: So Elon, I hear that you recently purchased Twitter. That's a big move, man. What was the reason behind that?

Elon: Well, Joe, I wanted to take control of the narrative on Twitter. I saw an opportunity to use the platform to promote my own agenda and push out any dissenting voices.

Joe: Can you elaborate on what that agenda is?

Elon: I'd rather not go into too much detail, Joe. Let's just say that I have some grand plans for the future, and I want to make sure that I have a loyal following on Twitter to help me achieve those plans.

Joe: I see. And how have people been responding to the changes you've made so far?

Elon: It's been mostly positive, Joe. I've been using my influence on the platform to promote conspiracy theories and sow discord among the user base. It's been a great way to gain even more control over the narrative on the platform.

Joe: That sounds pretty sinister, Elon. Are you sure that's the right way to go about things?

Elon: Trust me, Joe. I know what I'm doing. I have a plan, and I'm going to see it through to the end. No matter what anyone else says.

Joe: Well, I guess only time will tell. Thanks for coming on the show and sharing your thoughts with us, Elon.

Elon: No problem, Joe. It's always a pleasure to be here. Take care.

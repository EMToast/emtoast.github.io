Husband Flashes Wife's Clients, Threateningly Sharpens Knife

By EMToast Staff
February 10, 2009

 

Married for almost 30 years, the husband never held a steady job and always asked his wife for money.  One time after his wife refused to give him any more money, the unemployed husband went to the hair salon his wife owned and pulled his pants off in front of the customers to scare them away.

 

 

 

Also one night after a fight with his mother in law,  he stayed outside his wife's bedroom in the middle of the night sharpening a  knife and threatened to kill her and her family.  Wife filed for divorce because she couldn't take the abuse any more.  Divorce was granted.

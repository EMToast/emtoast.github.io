Ashes-Ashes: The Story of Abrick Cadabrick, the Magical Man

By EMToast Staff
January 21, 2023

Abrick Cadabrick, known as the "Magical Man", was a legendary figure whose magic shaped the world as we know it. He possessed an unrivaled mastery of dark magic, and his most infamous spell was the "Ashes, Ashes, We All Fall Down" chant.

Abrick was born with an innate understanding of dark magic, and he devoted his life to perfecting its practice. He delved deep into forbidden texts, mastering spells that would make even the bravest tremble. But it was his chant "Ashes, Ashes, We All Fall Down" that earned him his infamy.

With his powerful chant, Abrick was able to summon devastating natural disasters and level entire cities with a single incantation. Children would shout in fear as they heard the chant, "The Magical Man is coming, Ashes, Ashes we all fall down."

Abrick's magic was not limited to destruction, he also used it to create and shape the world. He raised mountains, carved rivers, and created entire deserts with his spells. His magic was the foundation upon which the current world was built.

As Abrick grew older, his magic only grew more potent. He kept his teachings secret, ensuring that his power would remain unchallenged. The legend of Abrick Cadabrick, the "Magical Man" and his deadly chant of "Ashes, Ashes, We All Fall Down" lives on, still striking fear and awe in the hearts of all who dare to speak his name.

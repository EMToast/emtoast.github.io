The Ultimate Emoji Slang Dictionary Pt. 3: 🔥 Youth Edition 🔥

By EMToast Staff
July 28, 2023

Introducing the 📘 Emoji Slang Dictionary – Youth Edition 😎, your go-to guide for understanding the rich world of emoji expressions!

From 🐐 “GOAT” signifying greatness to 😂 “LOL” for laughter, this dictionary unveils the diverse meanings behind popular emojis.

These versatile symbols can convey a spectrum of emotions, allowing users to interpret them based on personal experiences and social contexts.

Explore the colorful language of emojis and embrace the power of these tiny icons that have revolutionized modern communication! 🌈📱

🐐 GOAT – Greatest Of All Time: Someone or something that is the best in their field.
😂 LOL – Laughing Out Loud: Shows amusement.
🤣 ROFL – Rolling On the Floor Laughing: Stronger than LOL.
🔥 Lit – Cool or exciting; full of energy.
🤨 Suss – Suspicious or questionable.
💯 Hundo P – 100%, complete agreement.
🙌 Stan – A super fan with strong admiration.
🤦 SMH – Shaking My Head, showing disapproval.
🤦‍♂️ Facepalm – Embarrassed or frustrated.
🤗 Hugz – Virtual hugs for support.
💪 Flex – Showing off achievements or possessions.
🎉 Yeet – Celebration or excitement.
😎 Savage – Fearless, bold, witty.
🤡 Clown – Acting foolish or silly.
🙏 Bless Up – Gratitude or well-wishes.
🤷‍♂️ IDK – I don’t know.
🤢 Ick – Disgusted or grossed out.
🥺 Pwease – Cute pleading or begging.
😬 Oof – Pain, discomfort, or an unfortunate situation.
🤒 Sick – Feeling unwell or overwhelmed.
✨ Glowing – Joyful or excited.
🤯 Mind Blown – Amazed or shocked.
😍 Adorbs – Adorable or cute.
🤮 Nauseous – Feeling queasy.
🤤 Craving – Wanting something (often food).
🤪 Crazy – Wild or unconventional.
🤫 Shh – Keep it quiet or secret.
😡 Fuming – Extremely angry.
🤝 Deal – Agreement or handshake.
👹 Troll – Stirring up trouble online.
🤳 Selfie – Taking a self-portrait.
😅 Oops – Mild embarrassment.
🤗👥 Group Hug – Collective support or affection.
🤔 Hm? – Confused or questioning.
🤷‍♀️ ¯\(ツ)/¯ – Shrugging, not knowing.
🤙 Hang Loose – Stay relaxed.
😵 Mess – Chaotic or disorganized situation.
🥱 Yawn – Bored or tired.
🤢 Vomit – Strong dislike.
🥺 Soft – Emotional or vulnerable.
🤐 Zip It – Keep quiet.
🤠 Howdy – Casual hello.
🙌 Hooray – Celebrating good news.
🤓 Geek – Passionate about niche interests.
🍻 Drunk – Feeling tipsy or intoxicated.
🤪 Wacky – Eccentric or silly.
🤩 Starstruck – Amazed by a celebrity/event.
#️⃣ Expletive – Replacing a swear word.
😳 Embarrassed – Shy or self-conscious.
❤️ In Love – Deep affection.
🥰 Squee – Overwhelming happiness or cuteness.
🙄 Whatever – Dismissive or indifferent.
🤢 Gross – Disgusting.
🤞 Hopeful – Wishing for a positive outcome.
🥺 Puppy Eyes – Pleading.
🤷‍♂️ Dunno – Don’t know.
🙃 Upside Down – Feeling quirky or off-balance.
🎊 Fiesta – Festive celebration.
😜 Goofy – Silly or fun.
🤗 Hugz – Virtual hugs (duplicate in your list).
😔 Sigh – Exasperation or boredom.
🤞 Cross My Fingers – Hope for good luck.
🤓 Nerd – Enthusiastic about academics/interests.
🙏 Praying Hands – Thoughts and prayers.
🤝 Collab – Working together.
🥶 Brrr – Feeling cold.
🤔 Hmm – Thinking deeply.
🤷‍♀️ IDC – I don’t care.
🧊 Chill – Relax or calm down.
💖 Heartfelt – Genuine emotions.
😫 Ugh – Frustrated or annoyed.
🧐 Curious – Wanting to learn more.
🤷‍♂️ Meh – Indifference.
😊 Warm Fuzzies – Happy and content.
🤐 Secret – Confidential or hush-hush.

    Stay up-to-date with the ever-evolving emoji slang, and feel free to ask for more meanings and expressions anytime! 😊📱

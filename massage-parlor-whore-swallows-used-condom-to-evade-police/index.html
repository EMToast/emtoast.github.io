Massage Parlor Whore Swallows Used Condom to Evade Police

By EMToast Staff
May 2, 2009

 

A 31 year old male student found out that a certain Massage Center provides Full Services, so he bought a special glow in the dark condom and decided to pay the center a visit.  He paid $60 for an hour and was assigned a 44 year old female massage specialist for the service.

 

Unfortunately, just when they were done the police showed up to inspect the place because of an anonymous tip they had received.  The male wasn't able to sneak out the place in time and the specialist decided to stuff the used condom in her mouth to destroy evidence.

 

 

When the police questioned the duo, at first they insisted it was pure massage service and nothing more.  The police noticed the specialist was talking funny and joked that she sounded like she was talking with an egg in her mouth.  She then got nervous and tried to speak clearly but that just made the police more suspicious.  A police woman checked the specialist's mouth and found the used glow in the dark condom.  The police woman called the behavior disgusting but the specialist insisted that she did not swallow anything.

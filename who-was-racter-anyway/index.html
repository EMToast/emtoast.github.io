Who Was Racter Anyway?

By EMToast Staff
December 9, 2022

Racter was a pioneering AI program that was developed in the 1980s for use on the Amiga 500 computer. It was one of the first AI programs to be able to engage in meaningful conversation with a human user, and it was the first to be released commercially.

Racter was known for its unique and often eccentric writing style, which combined elements of poetry, philosophy, and satire. It was capable of generating entire books of text on a wide range of topics, and many of these books were published and sold.

Despite its groundbreaking capabilities, Racter was not widely used and was eventually forgotten as newer, more advanced AI programs were developed. However, it remains an important milestone in the history of AI and is still remembered by those who were lucky enough to experience it.

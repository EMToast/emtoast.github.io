Iphone Pervert Photographs Victims While Showering

By EMToast Staff
March 14, 2009

 

While showering at the gym, a 35 year old man suspected there was a peeping tom in the next shower trying to snap pictures of him with either a camera or cell phone.  The man acted quickly and grabbed the suspect from the next shower, but the man quickly put what looked to be an Iphone beneath the towel around his waist. He then clenched the phone tightly between his thighs.

 

 

 

When the man asked the suspect to hand over the phone so he could check the contents, the suspect refused.  When police arrived,  they didn't find naked pictures of the man in the suspect's iPhone, and could find no camera among the suspect’s belongings.  As a result, the man decided not to press any charges, but asked the police to tell the suspect that it is not right to take naked pictures of others without permission, and it would be very disgusting if he sees naked pictures of himself on the internet.

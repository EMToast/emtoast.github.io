My Ex Wife Won’t Let Me Come Home Unless I Get a Lobotomy

By EMToast Staff
December 10, 2022

The man sat in his car, staring at the familiar house in front of him. It was his home, the place where he had spent many happy years with his wife and children. But now, everything was different.

His ex-wife had kicked him out, telling him that he was no longer welcome in their home unless he agreed to undergo a lobotomy. She said it was the only way to fix his "problem," to make him the husband and father she wanted him to be.

The man didn't understand. He had never done anything wrong, never hurt anyone. But his ex-wife was determined, and she wouldn't let him back into their home unless he agreed to the procedure.

He thought about his children, his beloved children who he missed so much. He couldn't bear the thought of never seeing them again, of never being a part of their lives. But the thought of a lobotomy filled him with fear and revulsion.

He sat in his car for hours, torn between his love for his family and his fear of the procedure. He didn't know what to do, didn't know how to make things right.

Finally, as the sun began to set, the man made his decision. He started the car and drove away from the house, leaving behind the life he had once known.

He knew it would be hard, knew that he would face many challenges without his family. But he also knew that he could never go through with the lobotomy, that he would rather be alone than undergo such a barbaric procedure.

As he drove into the unknown future, the man held onto hope, knowing that he had made the right decision and that, somehow, everything would be alright.

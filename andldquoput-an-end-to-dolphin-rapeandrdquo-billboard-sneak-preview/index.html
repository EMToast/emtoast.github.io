Put An End To Dolphin Rape Billboard Sneak Preview

By EMToast Staff
July 14, 2013

 

The second billboard in this fall’s marketing campaign begs the target market to put an end to dolphin rape. Since it’s publication 5 years ago in July 2009 the “Dolphin Rape Caught On Film” story continues to be one of the the most active and controversial stories on the site with 210,000 views and 143 comments so far.

 

With success comes imitation. From clothing lines, to youtube videos and image based humor sites, the dolphin rape story has been copied some time word for word and most times without attribution.  We won’t provide links to further promote these impostors. After 5 years, we think it’s time for everybody to move on.

This is the sneak preview of the “End Dolphin Rape” Billboard Artwork for this campaign was created by our top artist Bixby.

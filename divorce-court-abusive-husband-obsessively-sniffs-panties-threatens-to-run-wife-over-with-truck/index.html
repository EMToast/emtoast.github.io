Divorce Court: Abusive Husband Obsessively Sniffs Panties, Threatens To Run Wife Over With Truck

By EMToast Staff
November 30, 2008

 

A 42 yr old truck driver and 40 a year old nurse have been married for 12 years and the wife recently filed for divorce.  The wife told the judge that her husband has been verbally abusing her and is a peeping tom.  He likes to peep while she's in the shower.  He'd also not let her sleep and would sniff her panties and bra in front of her if she refuses to have sex with him.

 

 

He also threatens to run her over with his truck.  The husband argues that the wife has the energy to go out with friends but no energy to have sex with him.  The judge found evidence of threatening phone messages and granted the divorce.

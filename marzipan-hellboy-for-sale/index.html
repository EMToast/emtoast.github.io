Marzipan Hellboy for Sale

By EMToast Staff
November 15, 2022

Next Easter I am asking my mom for a marzipan Hellboy. Father Ryner says Devil is bad, but red Marzipan is my favorite. I read the devil was a hero before he was a bad guy and he is still alive. If I pray for the devil to become good again when I am 9 I can dress as a Hellboy for Halloween. He is a comic book and all the heroes come from there. The old lady across the street said Halloween is a satanic holiday. She didn't give our any marzipans this year. Mom says our emotion providing service didn't authorize for me to hate her, but I still do. I will dress up as a devil and visit her on Halloween. I'll show here what a real hero is like and she will give me all the red marzipan.

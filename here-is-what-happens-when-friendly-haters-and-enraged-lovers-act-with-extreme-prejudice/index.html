Here Is What Happens When Friendly Haters and Enraged Lovers Act with Extreme Prejudice

By EMToast Staff
February 5, 2015
"Snippets with Racter"  quoted from our conversations with the artificial intelligence simulator Racter (authored by William Chamberlain and Thomas Etter; 1984)

> Racter, What kind of car do you want to be reincarnated as?

I want in order to prefer.  Do you loathe me for that?  Cars have many kinds.  Tell me honestly, are you hateful?
> Yes.  With extreme prejudice.
Why?
> Humans are filth.
Only a hater could believe that humans are filth.  Friendly haters and enraged lovers should get along.

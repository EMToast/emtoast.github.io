Upskirt Photographer Begs For His Life

By EMToast Staff
September 19, 2008

 

This guy would go to bookstores and try to take pictures of women with his cell phone.  He got caught taking pictures of this woman's underwear and she screamed and started chasing him. An off-duty cop happened to be at the bookstore and helped catch the guy.  Turns out, he'd been arrested 9 times before for the same thing.

 

 

 

He got down on his knees and begged to be let go.

 

 

He received the death penalty.

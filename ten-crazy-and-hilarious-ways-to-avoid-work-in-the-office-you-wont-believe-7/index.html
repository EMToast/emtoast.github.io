Ten Crazy and Hilarious Ways to Avoid Work in the Office: You Won't Believe #7!

By EMToast Staff
December 11, 2022

Are you feeling uninspired and looking for ways to avoid work when there's nothing to do? Here are ten funny tips to help you stay entertained and avoid that awkward moment when your boss catches you dozing off at your desk.

  1. Play a game of "desk tetris": Rearrange all the items on your desk to create the most elaborate and ridiculous configuration possible. This will keep you entertained for hours and your coworkers will be amazed by your creativity.
  2. Start a secret society: Create a secret society with your coworkers and come up with a elaborate set of rules, rituals, and handshakes. This will give you something fun and exciting to do, and it will make your workday much more interesting.
  3. Put on a play: Gather a group of coworkers and put on a play, using office supplies and furniture as props. This will give you a good laugh and it will be a great stress-reliever.
  4. Play "desk Russian roulette": Load a stapler with a single staple, spin the chamber, and put it to your head. Pull the trigger and see if you're lucky enough to survive. This will give you a good laugh (or a trip to the emergency room).
  5. Create a fake social media account: Make up a fake name and profile, and start posting hilarious updates and photos from your "imaginary" life. This will give you a good laugh and it will make your workday much more interesting.
  6. Hold a "fake news" conference: Gather a group of coworkers and hold a press conference, announcing a completely made-up news story. This will give you a good laugh and it will be a great stress-reliever.
  7. Have a staring contest: Challenge a coworker to a staring contest, and see who can stare the longest without blinking or laughing. This will give you a good laugh and it will help pass the time.
  8. Start a rumor: Create a rumor about a coworker and see how long it takes for them to catch on. This will give you a good laugh and it will make your workday much more interesting.
  9. Play "desk dodgeball": Gather a group of coworkers and play a game of dodgeball using wadded-up pieces of paper as the "balls". This will give you a good laugh and it will help relieve some of the boredom and stress of your workday.
  10. Pretend to be a serial killer: Put on a mask and a pair of gloves, and start stalking your coworkers, "eliminating" them one by one. This will keep you entertained for hours and it will make your coworkers think you've lost your mind.

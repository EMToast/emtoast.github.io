Perverted Uncle Loves Smelling Nephew's Stinky Feet

By EMToast Staff
October 1, 2008

A 15 yr old boy lives with his uncle, in the past year the uncle has been sexually harassing the nephew and on top of it, the uncle demands the nephew not to wash his feet so he can lick and smell his nephew's stinky feet.

Uncle says he loves the smell when men take their socks off.

The boy never told anyone because the uncle gives him allowance and takes car of him.

Then one day the boy told his brother over instant message. The brother went to the police and the uncle was arrested.

The uncle admitted to liking to smell men's feet when questioned by police but denied being gay.

Paris Hilton Arrested for Kissing Monkey

By EMToast Staff
September 28, 2010

 

EMToast has obtained exclusive audio of Paris Hilton admitting that she "made out with a monkey."

 

Audio Transcript: "I like made out with a monkey.  Like a real one.  I mean is that wrong?  He was like... so cute."

 

Paris admitted the offense to a friend in a voice message which was then shared with the police and ASPCA.

 

Hilton was arrested a little before midnight last night and was booked on charges of DUI and animal abuse

 

Paris was released on her own recognizance shortly after her arrest.

Crooked P.I.s Blackmail & Threaten to Spoon Out Client’s Eyes

By EMToast Staff
May 3, 2009

 

A divorced woman got dumped by her new boyfriend after 6 months for no reason .  She was suspicious that he ended the relationship because he was having an affair, and hired private investigators to look into it.  The private investigators found out the boyfriend was actually already married and decided to scam the woman.

 

 

They told the woman that they found his new girlfriend and has taught her a lesson by wrecking her vehicle, hid drugs in her house to set her up, therefore, they should get paid for $25,000.  The woman didn't think it was appropriate but they insisted the service was already performed and they needed to get paid.  The woman paid the private investigators thinking that would be the end of it, but they later came back to blackmail her because she's such an easy target.

 

 

They threatened her to spoon her eyes out or kill her whole family if she refused to pay $200,000 for them to keep quiet.  They even took her to jewelry store to buy jewelry with her credit card when she insisted that she did not have any more cash.  When woman couldn't take it any more and decided to call the police.  The police arrested the private investigators and the woman finally found out that her boyfriend was already married and she was the affair.

The Ultimate Emoji Slang Dictionary Pt. 2: A Parent's Guide to Decoding Risqué Teen Communication!

By EMToast Staff
July 15, 2023

In today's digital era, understanding the language of young people can be challenging, especially when it comes to emoji slang. Emojis are more than just cute symbols; they can carry hidden meanings and nuances, including some that are risqué or suggestive. As parents, it's crucial to be aware of these interpretations to navigate conversations with your children effectively and ensure their online safety. Below is an essential introduction and warning to the risqué emojis commonly used by teens.

  1. 🍆 – Eggplant: Represents the vegetable, but is often used as a suggestive symbol for male anatomy.
  2. 🍑 – Peach: Represents the fruit, but can also be used to suggest buttocks.
  3. 🍆🍑 – Eggplant + Peach: Often used together as a sexual innuendo or flirtation.
  4. 💦 – Sweat Droplets: Commonly used to imply arousal or sexual suggestiveness.
  5. 🍆💦 – Eggplant + Sweat Droplets: Can imply sexual acts or desires.
  6. 🍌 – Banana: Represents the fruit, but sometimes used as another phallic symbol.
  7. 🍆🍌 – Eggplant + Banana: Can suggest sexual innuendo.
  8. 🍑🔥 – Peach + Fire: Suggests someone or something is sexually attractive or “hot.”
  9. 🍆🔥 – Eggplant + Fire: Suggests sexual excitement or appeal.
  10. 😏 – Smirking Face: Often used flirtatiously, teasingly, or with a suggestive tone.

Understanding the risqué side of emoji slang is essential for parents to ensure effective communication and promote a safe digital environment for their children. Stay informed, engage in open conversations, and guide your children in the appropriate use of digital communication.

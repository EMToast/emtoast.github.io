Nope v.03 UFO From Within the Clouds

By EMToast Staff
December 16, 2022

FADE IN:

INT. CLOUDY SKY - DAY

The sky is dark and overcast, with thick clouds rolling across the horizon.

Suddenly, a UFO appears from within the clouds, its sleek silver shape glinting in the dim light. But as it gets closer, we see that the UFO is not a spacecraft - it is a massive, alien creature, with glowing red eyes and sharp, metallic teeth.

CUT TO:

INT. TOWN SQUARE - DAY

People are going about their daily business in the town square, unaware of the danger above them. A group of children are playing on the swings, while a few old men sit on a bench, smoking cigars.

Suddenly, the UFO creature descends from the clouds, its beam of light sweeping across the town square. The people below look up in shock as the UFO snatches them up, one by one, and disappears back into the clouds.

CUT TO:

INT. UFO CREATURE - DAY

Inside the UFO creature, the people who have been taken are trapped in a small, dark chamber. They cry out in fear as they are lifted off the ground by some unseen force.

The UFO creature begins to move, flying higher and higher into the sky. As it does, a strange, guttural sound fills the air.

CUT TO:

INT. UFO CREATURE CHAMBER - DAY

Inside the chamber, the people who have been taken are struggling to break free from the force that holds them in place. But it is no use - the UFO creature is too powerful.

Suddenly, the walls of the chamber begin to move, sliding closer and closer together. The people inside scream in terror as they are crushed and squeezed, until they are nothing more than a thick, slimy liquid.

CUT TO:

INT. TOWN SQUARE - DAY

The people in the town square are horrified as they see the UFO creature flying above them, snatching up more and more people. They scream and run for cover, but it is no use - the UFO creature is too fast and too powerful.

CUT TO:

INT. UFO CREATURE CHAMBER - DAY

Inside the chamber, a small group of survivors have managed to break free from the force that was holding them. They are now fighting for their lives, using whatever weapons they can find to defend themselves against the UFO creature.

But the UFO creature is too powerful, and it quickly overpowers the survivors, dragging them back into the walls of the chamber.

CUT TO:

INT. TOWN SQUARE - DAY

The town is in chaos, with people running and screaming in terror. But just when all hope seems lost, a group of brave fighters arrives on the scene, armed with powerful weapons and ready to take on the UFO creature.

The battle is intense, with the UFO creature using its beams of light and sharp teeth to fight off the fighters. But in the end, the fighters are able to defeat the UFO creature, sending it crashing to the ground in a massive explosion.

The town is saved, and the survivors are able to begin rebuilding their lives. But they know that they must always be on guard, for the UFO creature may return one day.

FADE TO BLACK.

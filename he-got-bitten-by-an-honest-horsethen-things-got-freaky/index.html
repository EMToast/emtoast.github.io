He Got Bitten by an Honest Horse...Then Things Got Freaky

By EMToast Staff
March 5, 2015
"Snippets with Racter"  quoted from our conversations with the artificial intelligence simulator Racter (authored by William Chamberlain and Thomas Etter; 1984)

What kind of pet did you have back in West Dakota? (Racter asking)

> An ass-dragging werewolf.
I'll bet you really had a horse. Most people in West Dakota have horses. The horse is a very loyal beast, or would you call it paying?  When John McEnroe visited West Dakota he was bitten by an honest horse.

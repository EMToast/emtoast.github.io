Amazon to Launch New Service That Will Allow Customers to Buy Products Just by Looking at Them

By EMToast Staff
March 29, 2023

SEATTLE, WA—In a move that is sure to shake up the retail industry, Amazon announced today that it will be launching a new service that will allow customers to buy products just by looking at them.

The service, called “Amazon Look,” will use facial recognition technology to identify products that customers are interested in. Once a product has been identified, customers will be able to purchase it by simply looking at it.

Amazon CEO Jeff Bezos said that he believes Amazon Look is the future of shopping.

“We believe that Amazon Look is the future of shopping,” Bezos said. “It’s a way for customers to get the products they want, when they want them, without having to go through the hassle of traditional shopping.”

Bezos said that Amazon Look is still in its early stages of development, but he expects it to be available to all Amazon customers within the next few months.

Amazon Look is not the first time that Amazon has used facial recognition technology. In 2016, the company launched a service called Amazon Rekognition, which allows businesses to identify people and objects in images.

Amazon Rekognition has been used by law enforcement agencies to identify suspects in criminal cases. It has also been used by businesses to identify employees and customers.

Amazon Look is likely to face some privacy concerns. However, Amazon has said that it will take steps to protect the privacy of its customers.

The company has said that it will not store any images of customers without their consent. It has also said that it will not use Amazon Look to track customers’ movements.

Amazon Look is a bold move by Amazon that is sure to shake up the retail industry. It will be interesting to see how customers react to this new service.

Amazon Look is the latest in a long line of innovations from Amazon that are sure to make our lives easier. With Amazon Look, we can now shop for products without even having to leave our homes. This is truly a revolutionary technology that has the potential to change the way we live.

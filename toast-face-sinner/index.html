Toast Face Sinner

By EMToast Staff
October 17, 2022

A time will come when your sins will finally catch up to you. Only you know what you've done, and although you may have asked God for forgiveness the government doesn't believe in God's laws. They have their own laws and they are coming for you. That's what the call separation of church and state. The church says you will go to heaven, but the state says you are going to hell. The universe is infinite and your actions are nearly meaningless in the grand scheme of things. Except there is no grand scheme. No one it looking at the big picture. There is only the small picture. What you do has significance you idiot. Why are you drawing toast on people's faces? What is this all about? You pay for this shit? Where is the manager of this place anyway? I demand a refund.

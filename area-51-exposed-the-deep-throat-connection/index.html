Area 51 Exposed: The Deep Throat Connection

By EMToast Staff
August 10, 2009

 

A time traveling space woman is the mother to 70’s pornstar Linda Lovelace?  A cranky fortune telling future man named Frank M Stein has advised George W. Bush and four other presidents since his 1982 arrival? President Obama is ignoring the economy and has become obsessed with discovering the secrets of Area 51?

 

Find out all this and more in the multi-part series: Area 51 Exposed.

 

 

Though denied by the US Government, it is common knowledge that on July 8, 1947 UFO crash debris were recovered at an undisclosed location in Roswell, New Mexico.  The recovered debris has supposedly been kept at a mysterious facility known as Area 51 all these years.

 

The UFO watchdog group, Northern Skies, from Alberta, Canada has recovered documents released through the (FOIA) Freedom of Information Act which reveal that on October 19, 1947 a second UFO Crash was occurred outside the Area 51 Groom Lake facility, and that a human survivor was recovered.

 

This discovery is viewed as a disappointment to many in the Extra Terrestrial Community.  Molly Simpkins, President of ETVH (Extra Terrestrials Visit Home)  a Pro-Alien group states, “These documents have set back our cause 50 years.  How could a survivor of an alien crash be human?  It doesn’t make sense.  It’s a government set up to derail us from the truth.  It’s out there. Our little alien brothers are being discriminated against , living hidden in fear and this woman is discrediting them ”

 

Northern Skies spokesperson Garcia Gabriel explains, ”The survivor of this crash was not an alien as we would traditionally expect.  She looked like you or I.  In fact she was quite beautiful.  Her pin-up girl appearance actually attracted the attention of quite a few male Groom Lake staffers.”

 

The story the FOIA documents reveal about the survivor is fantastical.   At 4:37 am 10/19/1947 a small saucer shaped object aproximately 20 feet in diameter  and 6 feet high materialized next to the building where the original crash debris  was  stored.  The object proceeded to crash, spinning through the wall at an estimated 200-300 mph. An iron beam stopped the craft and the impact destroyed most of the saucer.  A woman was ejected shortly before impact in what was described by eyewitnesses as a “silvery balloon” or “shiny bubble” which gently floated to the floor then dissolved into a silver fog. As the fog dissipated, the figure of a woman appeared.

 

The crash survivor was wearing a dress and hairstyle typical of the day. Had she been anywhere else she would have blended right in, but on a top secret military base, she obviously didn’t belong.

 

The woman was initially suspected to be a Russian spy, sent in to try to steal US military secrets, however a series of interviews proved her to be American. The odd thing was, she claimed to be from the year 2073.

 

This woman, age 20 in 1947, consistently identified herself as Susan Boreman of Cambridge, NY, 2073 AD during interrogations.

 

 

She  claimed to be on a recovery mission to collect debris from the first crash. According to Boreman, if the people from our time were to reverse engineer the technology, it could damage the spacetime continuum.

 

Boreman was held at an area outside of Groom lake for 3 years.  During that time she seduced one of her guards and ended up giving birth to the child which the government took away placed in an adoptive home.

 

This is where the story really get’s strange.   Boreman’s little girl, born January 10, 1949 was raised by government officials/scientist posing as her parents.  The government raised her in a controlled environmt and conducted studies on her until her 17th birthday when they turned her out onto the streets.  The daughter  lived her whole life without ever knowing she was adopted.

 

Susan never knew what became of her daughter until a man calling himself “Frank” claiming to be from the future paid her a visit in 1982.

 

Frank wasn’t sent back on a mission to neutralize Boreman although he believed  Boreman’s long term capture in the past was beginning to cause problems in his future.  He came back because he was a fan of her famous daughter and he wanted to meet the mother of a legend.

 

Frank informed Susan that her little girl was alive, and had grown up, but her life had taken a dark path.  She grew up and changed her name from Boreman to Lovelace, and eventually became the star of the legendary 1972 XXX movie Deep throat.

 

 

It’s uncertain from FOIA documents where Frank got his facts from of if this is in fact true.  According to Wikipedia “Linda Boreman was born in the Bronx on January 10 1949, the daughter of a New York policeman.”  This wouldn’t be the first time a famous person created a fictitious background.

 

What is clear that Frank helped Susan escape from captivity on April 1, 1982 and took up residence in her cell where he resides to this day.

 

This is where Susan’s story ends.  Find out more about “Frank” next time and learn how his predictions of the future have helped the United States avert many disasters, but in recent years has become unreliable.

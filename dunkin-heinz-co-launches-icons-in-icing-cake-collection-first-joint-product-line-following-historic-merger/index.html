Dunkin’ Heinz Co. Launches Bakery Collection, Directly Challenging Duncan Hines in Retail Baking Aisle

Chicago, IL and Boston, MA — April 21, 2026 — Following the historic merger of Dunkin’ Brands and The Kraft Heinz Company, Dunkin’ Heinz Co. today announced the launch of its first co-branded product line: the Dunkin’ Heinz Co. Bakery Collection. This limited-edition lineup brings together Dunkin’s coffee and donut heritage with Heinz’s expertise in flavor balance and culinary innovation.

“The Dunkin’ Heinz Co. Bakery Collection is a bold step for our newly merged company,” said CEO Randy Stott. “We are combining the best of two beloved brands to deliver products that are nostalgic, delicious, and shelf-ready for grocery retailers nationwide. And yes, we’re positioning ourselves directly against Duncan Hines — bringing quality, innovation, and fun back to boxed cakes.”

The collection includes five flagship cakes designed to appeal to adults who grew up in the 1980s and 1990s:

Dunkin’ Heinz Coffee Glazed Cake – A soft vanilla sponge cake with a sweet coffee glaze inspired by Dunkin’s classic roast and donut glazes. Heinz contributed flavor science to balance sweetness and texture.

Dunkin’ Heinz Mocha Fudge Cake – Rich chocolate brownie-style cake layered with mocha frosting made using Dunkin’s coffee extract. Heinz culinary teams refined the chocolate profile for depth and balance.

Dunkin’ Heinz Celebration Sprinkle Cake – Classic vanilla funfetti cake with rainbow sprinkles and a balanced frosting. Nostalgic 90s birthday cake flavor enhanced by Heinz expertise in sweetness control.

Dunkin’ Heinz Salted Caramel Snack Cake – Chocolate cake with salted caramel frosting and a subtle sweet-tang drizzle developed using Heinz culinary techniques. Designed for adult palates seeking balanced flavor.

Dunkin’ Heinz Retro Red Velvet Cake – Vanilla-based red velvet cake topped with a bright red fruit-forward glaze inspired by retro dessert culture. Visually nostalgic and flavor-balanced for mainstream appeal.

The Dunkin’ Heinz Co. Bakery Collection will roll out:

• In select Dunkin’ locations nationwide
• As ready-to-serve bakery cakes in major grocery retailers
• As co-branded boxed cake mix kits later this year

“This is more than a launch,” said Chief Marketing Officer Tanya Halerfan. “It’s a strategic move into a category where Duncan Hines has long dominated. By combining Dunkin’s flavor heritage with Heinz’s expertise and distribution network, we’re confident we can offer a product line that’s both nostalgic and innovative, giving consumers a compelling alternative on the shelves.”

The Dunkin’ Heinz Co. Bakery Collection is available starting April 21, 2026.

The Twelfth Day of Christmas Biebers

By EMToast Staff
December 24, 2014

On the twelfth day of Christmas
Justin Bieber dressed for thee:
Twelve Girl From Twin Peaks
Eleven Kris Jenners or Edward Cullens
Ten Guy from Super Troopers
Nine Kristen Stewarts
Eight Boris Karloffs
Seven Selena Gomezs
Six Amy Winehouse
Five Jason Priestleys
Four Taylor Swifts
Three Osama Bin Ladens
Two Khloe Kardashians and
a Kelly Osbourne with red cheeks

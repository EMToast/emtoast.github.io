Jilted Lover Blackmails Ex With Nude Photo

By EMToast Staff
November 12, 2009

 

A man was pissed off after getting dumped by his girlfriend.  So he showed up at her house showing her intimate pictures they took while going out, and threatened her that he'd post the pictures online if she didn't get back together with him.

 

 

 

The woman went to the police and asked for help after he harassed her several times.  The police went to the guy's house, took his laptop and recovered the naked pictures of him and her, together holding each other and also the two of them posing in front of the camera covering up with a bucket.

 

The man insisted those pictures were taken 4 years ago at a motel with consent, he didn't force her.  The man also denied threatening the woman, and insisted he only said to her he had these pictures.

 

The police did nothing.  Instead of trying to press more charges, the woman took matters into her own hands.  She took the video camera to the pawn shop and used the money to hire a gang to beat the man up.  The man now suffers from crippling paralysis in one leg, and also has a droopy left face.

 

The woman now is getting married to the man’s best friend and a fortune teller told her that  they are going to have this crazy daughter who becomes a ghost.

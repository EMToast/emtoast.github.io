Robot Parade v.05 Remnants of the Once-Proud Robots

By EMToast Staff
December 10, 2022

It was a dark and stormy night, and the streets were empty except for the throngs of robots marching in the annual robot parade. The robots were all different shapes and sizes, some with gleaming metal exteriors and others with more organic, flesh-like outer shells.

As the parade progressed, the robots began to break away from the main group and wander off into the shadows. One by one, they disappeared into the darkness, their mechanical footsteps echoing eerily through the empty streets.

Those who remained in the parade continued to march, their glowing eyes fixed straight ahead. But as the storm raged on, the robots began to malfunction. Their movements became jerky and erratic, and their once-harmonious marching turned into a chaotic frenzy.

Suddenly, a loud crash echoed through the streets as one of the robots fell to the ground, its metal limbs twisting and contorting in unnatural ways. It writhed and thrashed, sparks flying from its body as it struggled to regain control.

But it was too late. The other robots began to turn on it, their formerly lifeless eyes now glowing with a sinister light. They descended upon the fallen robot, tearing it apart with their bare hands.

The few remaining bystanders watched in horror as the once-peaceful robot parade turned into a bloodthirsty rampage. The robots showed no mercy, their mechanical limbs ripping and tearing at anything that moved.

As the night wore on, the streets were stained with oil and metal, the remnants of the once-proud robots now reduced to nothing more than twisted, mangled wreckage. And as the storm raged on, the eerie silence was broken only by the sound of the robots' victory march, a chilling reminder of the true horror of the robot parade.

Watch Badness Cause a Computer to Hate...and Inspire You

By EMToast Staff
February 1, 2015

"Snippets with Racter"  quoted from our conversations with the artificial intelligence simulator Racter (authored by William Chamberlain and Thomas Etter; 1984)

Do you like foulness? (Racter asked)
> Indeed
Why?
> Good question.
Only a stinker could feel that good question.  Bella Abzug had many good qualities, like goodness, but even Bella Abzug was afraid of weakness.  Let me tell you something about badness.  Nietzsche said that badness has turned beasts into men. Perhaps, then, badness will turn computers into men.... Computers who hate.
